ugali pork

a delicious ugali pork served with choped onions and tomatoes mixwd with pepper

ingredients

  • 2 cups of maize floor
  • 4 cups of water
  • a pinch of salt
  • 500g-1kg pork
  • 1-2 onions
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 bell pepper
  • cooking oil

instructions

  1. prep the pork
  2. fry the pork
  3. add onions
  4. add garlic& ginger
  5. add bell pepper
